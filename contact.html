What to Expect After You Reach Out

Our estimate process is quick, thorough, and completely pressure-free.

Step 1: Quick Conversation

We'll call or email within 24 hours to learn about your project — floor type, square footage, what you're envisioning, and your timeline. Takes about 5 minutes.

Step 2: Free On-Site Visit

We'll schedule a free visit to see your floors in person, assess the condition, discuss stain options, and provide a firm written quote.

Step 3: We Get to Work

Once you're ready, we schedule your project at a time that works for you and deliver floors that look like new — backed by our 3-year finish warranty.
