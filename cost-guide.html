Average Hardwood Floor Installation And Refinishing Costs in Spokane, WA

In Spokane, WA, typical hardwood floor installation ranges from $7 to $25 per square foot, encompassing materials and labor. For standard solid unfinished oak, you might expect $7-$12/sq ft. Premium prefinished or engineered wood, especially exotics or wider planks, generally falls between $10-$18/sq ft, while intricate patterns or very rare woods can push beyond $20-$25/sq ft. Hardwood floor refinishing, which revitalizes existing floors, typically costs $3.50-$5.50 per square foot for a standard sand, stain, and three coats of polyurethane. Projects requiring extensive repairs, custom staining, or specialized low-VOC finishes can reach $5.50-$8+ per square foot. These figures are influenced by the wood type, desired finish, and the complexity of the job, ensuring a tailored investment for your Spokane home.

Factors That Affect Hardwood Floor Installation And Refinishing Pricing in Spokane, WA

Several key factors influence the final cost of hardwood flooring services in Spokane, WA. The project's size is paramount; larger areas generally yield a lower per-square-foot cost due to economies of scale. The current condition of your existing floors significantly impacts refinishing prices, with extensive repairs or subfloor leveling adding to the overall expense. Material choice is another major differentiator: solid vs. engineered wood, domestic vs. exotic species, and wood grade all affect the per-square-foot price. Accessibility of the work area, such as stairs or tight spaces, can increase labor time. Finally, local Spokane labor costs, driven by demand for skilled craftsmen and operational overhead, play a crucial role in determining the ultimate project investment.
