About Us

About Us — Spokane Hardwood Floor Refinishing Specialists

Most Spokane homeowners dealing with worn or damaged hardwood floors have had the same frustrating experience: vague estimates, no-shows, or a refinishing job that peeled within a year. We built this business around a simpler idea — do the work right, quote it in writing, and stand behind it. We're not the cheapest option in the Inland Northwest. We are the option that shows up, communicates clearly, and delivers floors you'll actually be happy with.

What We Do

We specialize in hardwood floor refinishing, installation, and repair for Spokane and surrounding Inland Northwest communities. Our core work is floor refinishing — sanding, staining, and finishing residential and commercial hardwood floors. We also install solid and engineered hardwood and handle repairs ranging from water damage and board replacement to squeaky floor elimination. See all services.

What Makes Us Different

Written fixed-price quotes before any work starts: Every project gets a line-item written quote. We don't give verbal ranges and figure it out later. The price we quote is the price you pay — if we discover something unexpected under the floor, we stop and show you before any additional work proceeds.

Dustless sanding as standard — not an upgrade: Our HEPA-filtered sanding system captures dust at the source. Traditional refinishing without dust control distributes fine wood particles into Spokane's forced-air heating systems where they recirculate for months. Dustless is how we work every job, at no extra charge.

Inland Northwest climate expertise: Spokane's dramatic swings between dry heating-season winters and warm summers cause hardwood to move more than in most markets. We use finish systems, flexible gap fillers, and acclimation protocols calibrated for this specific climate. Coastal contractors and national chains don't understand this — we do.

Who We Serve

We serve homeowners and businesses across Spokane, Spokane Valley, Liberty Lake, Cheney, and surrounding Eastern Washington communities. We also serve select Idaho communities including Coeur d'Alene and Post Falls. If you're outside our primary area, call us — we may still be able to help.

Our Commitment

Every project gets a written quote before work starts. Every job is backed by a 3-year finish warranty covering adhesion failure and premature wear. If something unexpected comes up mid-project, we stop and talk to you before proceeding — you always know what you're paying for before it happens.
