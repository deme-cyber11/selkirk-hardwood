Whole-House Sand & Refinish

Our most requested service transforms tired, scratched, and faded hardwood floors back to their original beauty ‚Äî or better. Whether you own a 1920s Craftsman on South Hill with original fir floors or a 2010s Spokane Valley home with builder-grade oak, whole-house refinishing is the most cost-effective way to dramatically improve your home's look and value.

Our Refinishing Process

Every project begins with a thorough floor assessment. We check board thickness, identify problem areas like water damage or loose boards, and evaluate the existing finish. This ensures we choose the right sanding sequence and finish system for your specific floor.

  1. Preparation: We mask all baseboards, vents, and transitions. Doors are removed if needed. Nails are set below the surface.
  2. Coarse Sanding: 36-grit removes old finish and levels the floor. Decades of wear disappear.
  3. Medium & Fine Sanding: Progressive passes with 60-grit and 100-grit create a smooth, uniform surface.
  4. Staining (Optional): Your chosen color from 40+ options, with test patches applied first.
  5. Finish Application: Two to three coats of Bona Mega ONE waterborne polyurethane, with light buffing between coats.
  6. Final Inspection: Walk-through with you, written care guide, and 30-day follow-up check.

Premium Upgrade

Dustless Hardwood Floor Sanding

Traditional sanding fills your home with fine wood dust that settles on every surface and infiltrates HVAC ducts. In Spokane, where forced-air heating runs from October through April, that dust recirculates through your home for months. Dustless sanding eliminates this entirely.

How Our Dustless System Works

We use commercial-grade HEPA-filtered vacuum systems connected directly to every sanding tool via 4-inch hoses. The vacuums capture 99% of dust before it becomes airborne. HEPA air scrubbers run continuously to filter remaining particles down to 0.3 microns.

Benefits for Spokane Homes

  • No plastic sheeting needed ‚Äî save time and avoid sealed rooms
  • Stay in your home ‚Äî many families remain during dustless projects
  • Protect your HVAC ‚Äî Spokane furnaces run 6+ months a year; dust in your ducts recirculates constantly
  • Better finish adhesion ‚Äî fewer particles settling into wet finish means smoother results
  • Pet and allergy safe ‚Äî critical for Spokane households during allergy season
