Our Dustless Refinishing Process

  1. Inspection & preparation: We assess floor condition, identify problem areas (gaps, loose boards, pet damage), and prepare the space. HVAC vents are sealed to prevent dust infiltration.
  2. Dustless sanding: Our HEPA-filtered sanding equipment captures 99% of dust at the source. Three progressive passes achieve a perfectly smooth surface ready for stain. In Spokane, where forced-air heating runs 6+ months a year, dustless sanding prevents months of recirculating wood dust through your ductwork.
  3. Custom stain application: Choose from 40+ stain colors or go natural. We apply a sample board to confirm your color choice before committing to the entire floor.
  4. Three finish coats: Professional-grade polyurethane (oil-based or water-based) applied with light screening between coats for maximum adhesion and durability. We use finishes formulated for low-humidity environments to resist the cracking and peeling common in Spokane homes.
  5. Final walkthrough: We inspect the completed floor with you. Our 3-year finish warranty covers any defects.
