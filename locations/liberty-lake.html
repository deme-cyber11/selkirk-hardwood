Frequently Asked Questions

How much does hardwood refinishing cost in Liberty Lake?

Liberty Lake hardwood refinishing typically costs $4–$9 per square foot. Premium homes with larger floor plans (1,200–2,500 sq ft) usually run $5,000–$15,000 depending on finish choice and scope. Contact us for a free estimate.

Do you refinish engineered hardwood in Liberty Lake new builds?

Yes — many newer Liberty Lake homes feature engineered hardwood. We use specialized light-sanding techniques that refinish the wear layer without compromising the board structure. Engineered floors can typically be refinished 1–2 times depending on wear layer thickness.

What stain colors are popular in Liberty Lake homes?

Liberty Lake homeowners gravitate toward modern, sophisticated tones — whitewashed oak for Scandinavian-inspired interiors, weathered gray for contemporary designs, and rich walnut for transitional spaces. We bring our full 40+ color library and apply test patches in your home.

Is there a travel surcharge for Liberty Lake projects?

No — Liberty Lake is within our standard service area. No travel fees or surcharges for any project in Liberty Lake, including the River District, Meadowwood, and surrounding communities.
