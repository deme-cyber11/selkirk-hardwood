Frequently Asked Questions — Hardwood Floor Installation and Refinishing in Liberty Lake

How much does hardwood flooring cost in Liberty Lake?

Hardwood floor installation in Liberty Lake typically costs $7 to $14 per square foot for materials and labor, while refinishing runs $3 to $8 per square foot. Liberty Lake's newer upscale homes often feature larger open floor plans that can bring project totals to $8,000 to $16,000 for full installations. We provide free in-home estimates for all Liberty Lake properties.

What hardwood species do you recommend for Liberty Lake homes?

For Liberty Lake's newer construction, we frequently install wide-plank white oak, which pairs beautifully with the modern open-concept layouts common in Meadowwood and the town center area. Hickory is another excellent choice for its durability and character. For homes near the lake, we recommend engineered hardwood for superior moisture stability.

Do you work with Liberty Lake builders on new construction?

Yes. Liberty Lake continues to see new residential development, and we work directly with local builders and homeowners on hardwood installations during the construction finish phase. We handle subfloor preparation, proper acclimation of materials, and coordinate our timeline with other trades to keep your project on schedule.
