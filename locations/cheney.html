How much does hardwood refinishing cost in Cheney?

Cheney hardwood refinishing typically costs $3–$7 per square foot. Most projects fall in the $2,500–$5,500 range for a complete sand-and-refinish. Contact us for a free estimate.

Do you refinish rental properties near EWU campus?

Yes — we work with many Cheney landlords and property managers. Summer between academic years is the ideal time for refinishing. We offer quick turnaround scheduling to minimize vacancy time.
