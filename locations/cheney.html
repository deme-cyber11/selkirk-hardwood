Cheney is a charming college town of approximately 12,500 residents located 16 miles southwest of downtown Spokane. Home to Eastern Washington University, Cheney blends the energy of a university campus with the quiet character of a historic farming community founded in 1882. The nearby Turnbull National Wildlife Refuge provides residents with easy access to nature, while the walkable downtown along First Street anchors the local economy. Cheney's housing stock is uniquely diverse, ranging from beautifully maintained Victorian and Craftsman homes near the EWU campus that feature original old-growth hardwood floors, to mid-century family homes, to modern student-oriented housing. This variety means Selkirk Hardwood handles everything from delicate restoration of century-old fir and oak in historic properties to durable new installations built to withstand the higher foot traffic common in a college town rental market.

Frequently Asked Questions — Hardwood Floor Installation and Refinishing in Cheney

How much does hardwood flooring cost in Cheney?

Hardwood floor installation in Cheney typically costs $6 to $11 per square foot for materials and labor, while refinishing runs $3 to $7 per square foot. Cheney's mix of historic homes near downtown and newer construction keeps project costs between $3,000 and $10,000 for most homeowners. We provide free in-home estimates with no obligation.

Can you refinish original hardwood in older Cheney homes?

Absolutely. Many of Cheney's historic homes near the Eastern Washington University campus and along First Street feature original fir, oak, and maple hardwood floors from the early 1900s. These old-growth floors are typically three-quarter inch thick and can be refinished multiple times. We assess board condition and thickness before starting any restoration project.

Do you serve rental properties and landlords in Cheney?

Yes. As a college town, Cheney has a large rental market, and we work with landlords and property managers on hardwood refinishing between tenants. We offer efficient turnaround times to minimize vacancy, and our durable commercial-grade finishes withstand the higher traffic typical of student rental properties.
