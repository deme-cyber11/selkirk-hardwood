Hardwood Floor Refinishing on Spokane's South Hill

Expert hardwood refinishing for the South Hill's beautiful historic and modern homes. NWFA certified, dustless sanding, 3-year warranty.

South Hill's Hardwood Flooring Experts

Spokane's South Hill is home to some of the most beautiful residential architecture in the Inland Northwest — from the stately Craftsman homes along Grand Boulevard to the mid-century gems near Manito Park. Many of these homes feature original hardwood floors that, with proper care, can last another century.

At Selkirk Hardwood, we've refinished hundreds of floors in the South Hill neighborhoods of Manito, Comstock, Cliff-Cannon, Lincoln Heights, and the Perry District. We understand the unique characteristics of South Hill homes — from the old-growth fir in 1920s bungalows to the red oak in 1960s ranchers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does hardwood refinishing cost on the South Hill?

South Hill hardwood refinishing typically costs $3–$8 per square foot, depending on floor condition, stain choice, and project size. Most South Hill homes with 800–1,200 sq ft of hardwood run $3,000–$7,000. Contact us for a free estimate.

Do you work with the original hardwood in older South Hill homes?

Absolutely. Many South Hill homes built in the 1920s–1950s have beautiful original fir or oak floors. We specialize in refinishing and restoring these heritage floors, preserving the character while giving them a fresh, durable finish.

How long does refinishing take for a typical South Hill home?

Most South Hill refinishing projects take 3–5 days. You can walk on your floors in socks after 24 hours and return furniture after 48 hours. Full cure takes about 14 days.

Do you offer dustless sanding on the South Hill?

Yes — dustless sanding is our standard. Our equipment captures 99%+ of sanding dust at the source, keeping your South Hill home clean during the refinishing process.
