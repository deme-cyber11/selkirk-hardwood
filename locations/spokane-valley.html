Hardwood Floor Refinishing in Spokane Valley

Professional hardwood refinishing for Spokane Valley's suburban homes and growing communities. NWFA certified, dustless sanding, 3-year warranty.

Spokane Valley's Trusted Hardwood Floor Team

Spokane Valley is the Inland Northwest's fastest-growing community, stretching from Millwood to Otis Orchards along the Spokane River corridor. With a mix of established 1970s–1990s ranch homes and newer construction near the Spokane Valley Mall, the Valley's housing stock features some of the most common hardwood flooring challenges we see — worn builder-grade oak, outdated honey-toned finishes, and floors that need a modern refresh to match today's open-concept living.

At Selkirk Hardwood, we've refinished hundreds of floors across Spokane Valley neighborhoods including Millwood, Opportunity, Greenacres, Veradale, and the University City area. We understand Valley homes — the larger floor plans that benefit from consistent stain color, the open layouts where kitchen and living room floors need to flow seamlessly, and the newer engineered hardwood that requires specialized sanding techniques.

Refinishing in Spokane Valley typically runs $3–$8 per square foot. Valley homes tend to have larger hardwood areas (1,000–1,500 sq ft), which means better per-square-foot pricing. Most whole-house projects cost between $3,500 and $8,000. See our complete Spokane pricing guide for details.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does hardwood refinishing cost in Spokane Valley?

Spokane Valley hardwood refinishing typically costs $3–$8 per square foot. Most Valley homes with 1,000–1,500 sq ft of hardwood run $3,500–$8,000 depending on floor condition and finish choice. Contact us for a free in-home estimate.

Can you refinish builder-grade oak floors in newer Spokane Valley homes?

Absolutely. Many Spokane Valley homes built in the 1990s–2010s have builder-grade red oak with a factory finish that's worn thin. We sand down to bare wood and apply professional-grade Bona finishes with custom stain colors that completely transform the look.

Do you serve the Millwood and Greenacres areas?

Yes — we serve all of Spokane Valley including Millwood, Greenacres, Opportunity, Otis Orchards, and Veradale. No travel surcharge for anywhere in the Valley.

How long does refinishing take for a Spokane Valley home?

Most Spokane Valley refinishing projects take 3–5 days. Open-concept layouts common in Valley homes allow us to work efficiently across large connected areas. Walk on floors in socks after 24 hours, furniture back after 48 hours.
