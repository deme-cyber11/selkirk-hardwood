Spokane Valley is the largest independent city in the Spokane metropolitan area, home to more than 106,000 residents spread across a thriving corridor east of downtown Spokane. Incorporated in 2003, the Valley stretches from Millwood along the Spokane River to Otis Orchards, with the bustling Sprague Avenue serving as its commercial spine. Landmarks like CenterPlace Regional Event Center and the scenic trails at Mirabeau Point Park anchor a community that blends suburban convenience with easy access to outdoor recreation. Spokane Valley homes range from established 1970s ranches with original hardwood to newer construction in master-planned communities.
