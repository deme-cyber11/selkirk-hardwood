Post Falls is one of the fastest-growing cities in North Idaho, with a population approaching 40,000 residents in western Kootenai County. New subdivisions continue to expand west of Highway 41 and south along Prairie Avenue, bringing hundreds of new homes to the market each year. This rapid growth means Post Falls homeowners need hardwood flooring professionals who understand both new construction installations and the refinishing demands of the city's more established neighborhoods near downtown and along the river.
